Easy Fried Okra

A Southern Classic that's quick and simple. Perfect as a snack or a side dish!

Ingredients

  • 10 pods of okra

  • 1 egg, beaten

  • 1 cup cornmeal

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

  • buttermilk(optional)

  • flour (optional)

Steps

  • Slice okra pods into 1/4 inch pieces.Soak okra pieces in the beaten egg for 5 to 10 minutes. For a stronger flavor, you can add a little buttermilk to the egg

  • In a medium bowl, combine cornmeal, salt, and pepper. Coat the okra in the cornmeal mixture evenly. Pro tip: You can cut the cornmeal with white flour to help the batter stick to the okra in the frying pan.

  • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place okra in hot oil, careful not to splash. Stir continuously and reduce heat when okra starts to brown. Cook until golden and drain on paper towels.
